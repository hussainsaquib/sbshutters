who are we :

S.B. Rolling Shutters & Engineering Works Incorporated in the year 1996, S.B. Rolling Shutters & Engineering Works has created a niche amongst trusted firms for manufacturing and supplying an extensive range of Rolling Shutter, Metal Grills, Metal Gate, Remote Control Rolling Shutter, Roof Trusses, Aluminium Door, Steel Railing & many more. We also provide Shed Fabrication Works to our valued clients. We are a Sole Proprietorship based company and customers trust us for offering quality approved products. We source superior quality material for manufacturing our products from leading vendors of the market. The products of our company are manufactured by our professionals and experts as per the specifications given by the customers and also in standards specifications. We test the products on various quality parameters before their delivery to the customers to ensure they are quality approved.

Situated in Hyderabad, Telangana, India, our infrastructure facilities help us to serve customers with our best. The infrastructure of our company consists of production, quality control, testing, warehouse, packaging and many more. The various units are handled in synchronization with each other so as to get best combined output. We have hired professionals, experts, engineers and many more people for handling work of different units as per their expertise. The various units are equipped with modern tools, machinery, equipment and many more that are necessary for executing activities of business.

With the support of Mr. Syed Zakiuddin Ahmed (Proprietor), our company has archived the topmost position in the market and in short span of time. His expert advice, skills, experience and diligence help in satisfying customers and provide market with unique and innovative products.
